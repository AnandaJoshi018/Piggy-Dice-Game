## ğŸ“Œ Game Rules

- The game has **2 players**, playing in turns.
- On a player's turn:
  - They can roll the dice as many times as they want.
  - Each roll adds to their **current score**.
  - If they roll a **1**, their current score becomes 0 and the turn switches.
- A player can choose to **Hold**, which:
  - Adds the current score to their total score.
  - Switches the turn to the other player.
- The first player to reach the winning score (default: **20**) wins the game.
